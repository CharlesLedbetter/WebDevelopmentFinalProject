Summer Camp

We offer three summer camp sessions each year, each with their own theme and emphasis. Each is three weeks long with food, housing, and robotics supplies included. In the first session the campers will build and race their own flying drones. In the second session campers work together in teams to engineer robots that can solve complex puzzles. In the Third and final summer camp session the campers build battle bots. Each camp is broken into age groups. Ages range from eight to seventeen. We can't wait to see your camper here!

Other Events

Camp Automaton is not just a summer camp for kids. We offer two adult sessions every year. One in the spring that focuses on building drone racers, and one in the fall where the campers build battle bots. We also have events through-out the year for nerds of all ages. These events include weekend meet-ups in our sponsored robotics clubs all over North America, Automaton Fairs where anyone can show off their robots and drones, and Drone Grand Prix events where first person drone racers compete to win it all. We want to encourage the world to build there own bots and drones. Everyone is welcome. Come join us in the fun!
