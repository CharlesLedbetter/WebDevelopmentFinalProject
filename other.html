Adult Camps

Spring and Fall Sessions

The spring and fall adult camps offer the fun of the summer camps tailored for those with a passion for robotics, still young at heart. The Spring session focuses on team work cognitive puzzle solving. Teams work together to build robots that can solve puzzles. The Fall session focuses on team work and competitive design. Teams work together to build a large scale battle robot. Each Session lasts four weeks, with all robotics materials and design software included. Each session culminates in a competition where the winners are given VIP passes passes to the Drone Grand Prix and the TocMax Robotics' International RoboBattle Royal.

The Automatons

Regional Robotics Clubs

Camp Automaton sponsors fourteen regional robotics clubs across North America and two in Japan. These clubs are open to anyone with an interest in robotics and drone racing. Many of these clubs host regional events like Automaton Fairs and robotics competitions. Some of the clubs specialize in drone racing and host the regional qualifiers for the annual Drone Grand Prix. we would love to have you in our international network of robotics clubs, so if you are interested please contact us.
